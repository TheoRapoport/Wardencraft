HOSTILE MOBS

EXAMPLES

What are zombies?

Zombies are common undead hostile mobs that deal melee damage and commonly attack in groups. If they spawn in the desert, they can be husks, that do not take damage from the sun. There is a 5% chance to spawn as zombie villagers and 5% to spawn as baby zombies.

What is a creeper?

A creeper is a common hostile mob that silently approaches players and then explodes, which can destroy blocks and kill unarmored players on any difficulty. When struck by lightning, a creeper becomes a charged creeper, which amplifies its explosion power. The creeper model is a fail to create a pig model, but the creator liked it and said "it looks creepier when animated".

What is a phantom?

Phantoms are flying undead hostile mobs that spawn in the night sky when the player has not laid in a bed or died for three or more in-game days. They attack by diving at the player from the sky to bite them. Usually, they appear in trios.

What is a boss?

Boss mobs are special hostile mobs that are distinctly more dangerous and tougher than other mobs. They do not spawn randomly, and are confronted intentionally. Boss mobs provide unique challenges but also equivalent rewards. There are 2 bosses: the Wither and the Ender Dragon.
