ENDER DRAGON

What is the Ender Dragon?

The ender dragon is a giant flying hostile boss mob found when first entering the End. It is the largest naturally spawning mob in the game. It guards access to the end gateways that provide entry to the outer end islands in addition to the exit portal that allows return to the Overworld as well as access to the end credits of Minecraft. Because of this, it is known by most Minecraft fans as the 'final boss' of the game.

How can you find it?

Using eyes of ender the player can find the stronghold, there is a portal with 12 end portal frames that if you place an eye in everyone of them, the portal works. The portal takes you to the end where you find the dragon. After killing it you can re-summon it by placing 4 end crystals on the edges of the exit portal (the bottom image on the right).

Why do you kill it?

The Ender Dragon is said as the 'final boss', so you need to kill it to say that you have beaten the game. After killing it you unlock a small flying portal (image on the top) that you can teleport to another part of the End, where you find the elytra (image on the bottom)and chorus fruits. The boss also gives advancments.
