NEUTRAL MOBS

EXAMPLES

What are piglins?

A piglin is a neutral mob found in the Nether. It is hostile to players unless they wear at least one piece of golden armor. If the player drops gold, they stop to attack the player and grab the item, giving something in return. It wields either a golden sword or a crossbow. Click the image to know more.

What are endermans?

The Enderman is a tall neutral mob found in all three dimensions. Endermen normally ignore players, but they will start to attack those that damage it or look directly at his eyes. They teleport to avoid water (which harms them), projectiles and some other damage sources, and occasionally pick up certain blocks.

What is a wolf?

A wolf is a neutral mob that can be tamed and used to assist in combat with most hostile mobs. If it is not tamed, if the player attacks one, other wolfs will attack the player, they also attack sheeps, rabbits, foxes, baby turtles and skeletons. You can tame them by giving a bone and if you hit a player or a mob, the wolf will help you.

What is an iron golem?

An iron golem is a buildable neutral mob that attacks monsters with its arms, knocking them into the air. Iron golems created by villagers or spawned in villages patrol their village, and may attack players that attack them. If one is damaged, you can heal it by gining an iron ingot.
