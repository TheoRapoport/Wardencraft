THE WITHER

What is the Wither?

The wither is a player-constructed and highly destructive undead hostile boss mob that can hover in mid-air and fire barrages of explosive wither skulls at its target (which happens to be everything living). It is the only source of nether stars, which are used to craft beacons. Some people say the Wither is the hardest mob to kill in minecraft.

How to build the Wither?

The wither is spawned by placing 4 blocks of soul sand or soul soil in a T shape, and putting 3 wither skeleton skulls on top of the three upper blocks. The skulls can be obtained by killing a wither skeleton with a 2.5% drop chance, with looting is increased 2% per level.

Why do you kill the Wither?

It is the only source of nether stars, which are used to craft beacons. Beacons are constructions that can buff all players in a 50x50 area, it buffs speed, strenght, haste and regeneration. You also get an ad
