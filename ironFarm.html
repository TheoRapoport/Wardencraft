IRON FARM

What is an iron farm?

Iron farms are used to give you iron without the need to go into a cave or melting it in a furnace. Every iron farm uses the fear of villagers to zombies, than a iron golem will spawn to defend them, by killing it, it drops iron ingots. Iron is used in many other farms, craftings and is the easiest to activate a beacon.

On the right is a video of a tutorial of how to easily build an iron farm. It works perfectly and can be made right when you find your first village, but I suggest you to do after the villager farm, so you have enough villagers to everything you need.
