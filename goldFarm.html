GOLD FARM

What is a gold farm?

Gold farms are used to give you gold without the need to mine, it is a late-game structure and take a lot of time to build, but it is worth. This farm uses the anger of zombie piglins to run after you, falling into a hole and dying, giving gold and xp. With gold you can barter with piglins, make golden apples and golden carrots, the best food in the game.

On the right is a video of a tutorial of how to build a gold farm. It works perfectly and can be made without any hard mechanics.
