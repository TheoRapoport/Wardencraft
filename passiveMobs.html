PASSIVE MOBS

EXAMPLES

What are villagers?

Villagers are passive mobs that inhabit villages, work, breed and interact with each other. Their outfit vary according to their occupation and biome, so as their houses and other structures. Villager is the most complex mob in Minecraft, having a lot of different behaviors. Click image to see more.

What is a pufferfish?

Pufferfish is a passive aquatic mob that can be found in oceans, you can get his food by fishing with a fishing rod, but it is poisonous. He is the only passive mob that can hurt you, but they will not follow you, they defensively inflate and if you get close it deals damage and inflicts poison.

What is a cat?

Cat is a passive mob that can spawn in villages and inside swamp huts. They share the same model, but different skins. Cats can be tamed with raw cod or raw salmon, if so, they can be sat and fed, while the player sleeps the cat will go near and has a chance to give you a gift. Cats are useful to scare creepers and phantoms.

What is a parrot?

Parrot is a tamable passive mob that spawns in jungle biomes. Just like cats, they share the same model, but can have different skins. Parrots can be tamed with seeds, to be sat and fed. They imitate sounds of nearby monsters and can perch on your shoulders.
