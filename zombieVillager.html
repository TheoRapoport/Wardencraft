ZOMBIE VILLAGER

What is a zombie villager?

Zombie villagers are variations of zombies, they look like green villagers. They have a 5% chance of spawning naturally, but in hard mode, if a villager is killed by a zombie he turns into a zombie villager. Every zombie villager can be healed into a normal villager.

How to heal zombie villagers:

To heal zombie villagers you need a Splash Potion of Weakness, made with the brewing stand using water bottles, 1 blaze powder for fuel, 1 fermented spider eye and gunpowder, you also need a golden apple, an apple in the middle and 8 gold ingots around. After giving this to the zombie villager it will start shaking and after a while turn to a normal villager. I recommend using the potion on a group to save materials.
