BARTERING WITH PIGLINS

Bartering

To barter with a piglin, either throw by dropping a gold ingot near an adult piglin or use a gold ingot on it. After the piglin takes the gold ingot and examines it for some seconds, it tosses a random item to the player. Piglin brutes ignore gold ingots and do not barter.
