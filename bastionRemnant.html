BASTION REMNANT

What is a bastion remnant?

Bastion remnants are large, castle-like generated structures found in the Nether. They generate in four distinct variants, each with a unique structure and set of loot. Piglins, piglin brutes and hoglins spawn in these structures. Bastion remnants are the only place to find gilded blackstone, the Pigstep music disc, the snout banner pattern and armor trim, the magma cube monster spawner, and the netherite upgrade smithing template.

Why do you go there?

Bastion remnants are the only place to find gilded blackstone, the Pigstep music disc, the snout banner pattern and armor trim, the magma cube monster spawner, and the netherite upgrade smithing template (needed for and netherite armor).
