VILLAGERS

What are villagers?

Villagers are passive mobs that inhabit villages, work, breed and interact with each other. Their outfit vary according to their occupation and biome, so as their houses and other structures. Villager is the most complex mob in Minecraft, having a lot of different behaviors.
